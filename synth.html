Sound Synthesizer

Based on the ideas of javidx9.

Use your keyboard to play piano! The keys on the lower row "z", "x", "c", ... correspond to the notes A, B, C, ... on the piano (white keys), and the keys on the middle row "s", "f", "g", ... correspond to the notes A#/Bb, C#/Db, D#/Eb, ... on the piano (black keys).
